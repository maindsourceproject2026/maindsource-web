A Tribute

The Woman Who
Inspired MAINDSOURCE

“She never told me what to build. She showed me what to stand for.”

Behind every purposeful venture is a guiding force. For Surya Kiran, Founder of MAINDSOURCE, that force has always been his mother — Smt. Varamahalakshmi. A woman of quiet strength, unshakeable values, and a deep belief in the potential of every person she encountered.

She taught him early that real leadership is not about authority — it is about responsibility. That no organisation deserves to call itself successful unless it lifts the people most often overlooked. Her words were not lectures. They were lived examples, shown every single day in how she treated people around her with dignity and care.

Her lifelong conviction in the power and capability of women became the moral foundation on which MAINDSOURCE was built. Every time she saw a woman working hard and not being given her due, she spoke up. She acted. And she raised a son who would carry that belief into every corner of his work.

The Women Empowerment agenda that sits at the very heart of MAINDSOURCE is not a policy statement or a marketing line. It is a living tribute — to her values, her vision, and her voice. A promise that the company she believed in would always stand for what she stood for.

Smt. Varamahalakshmi — The heart behind MAINDSOURCE
