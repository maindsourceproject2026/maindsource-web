Smt. Varamahalakshmi The Heart Behind MAINDSOURCE
A Mother's Legacy

She Taught Him That Every Woman Deserves a Place at the Table

"Behind every company that stands for something, there is a person who made the founder believe it was possible."

Smt. Varamahalakshmi is more than a mother to Surya Kiran — she is the foundation upon which MAINDSOURCE was built. Her wisdom, quiet strength, and unwavering belief in people taught him that every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, carries immense potential waiting to be recognised.

Her deep conviction that women are among the most capable, determined, and resilient members of any workforce became the moral compass of this company. She instilled in Surya not just the drive to succeed in business, but the purpose behind it — to open doors, create opportunities, and champion equality in every placement MAINDSOURCE makes.

The Women Empowerment mission at MAINDSOURCE is not a policy. It is a promise — the same promise she made every single day: that hard work, integrity, and belief in others can change lives.
