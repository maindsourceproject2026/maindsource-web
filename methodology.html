Our Process

A Process Built for Speed and Precision

From the moment you share a requirement to the moment a candidate walks through your door — every step is deliberate, structured, and accountable.

Lottie · Methodology Steps
1
Step One

Understand

We start by deeply understanding your requirement — not just the job description, but the work environment, team dynamics, and the kind of person who will succeed in this role.

Detailed requirement discussion with your HR or operations team
Understanding company culture and workforce expectations
Defining the ideal candidate profile — skills, experience, attitude
2
Step Two

Source

We activate our sourcing network across Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, and Telangana — reaching candidates through multiple channels simultaneously for fastest turnaround.

Internal database of 8,000+ pre-screened candidates
Active field sourcing through community networks
Digital platforms and local outreach for blue-collar talent
3
Step Three

Screen

Rigorous multi-layer screening ensures only the most suitable candidates reach your desk — saving you time and reducing hiring mistakes.

Initial telephonic screening against your defined criteria
Document verification — ID, address, work history
Skill assessment and attitude evaluation
Background checks where required
4
Step Four

Match

The right person at the right place at the right time — our matching step aligns verified candidates with your specific requirement before any introduction is made.

Tech-enabled candidate-role alignment scoring
Shortlist presentation with detailed candidate profiles
Client walk-in or interview coordination
5
Step Five

Deploy & Support

Our commitment does not end at placement. We stay engaged to ensure smooth onboarding and long-term retention of every candidate we place.

Joining coordination and documentation support
First 30-day check-in with placed candidates
Replacement support in case of early attrition
