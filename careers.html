Application Process

How to Apply — Simple & Fast

Our entire process from application to deployment takes under 72 hours. Here’s exactly what happens.

1

Browse & Click "View Details"

Find a job above that matches your skills and location. Click "View Details" to see the full job description, salary, shift timings, vacancies and benefits.

2

Click "Apply via Official Portal" or WhatsApp

Each job has a dedicated apply link. Click it to submit your details through our secure form — name, phone, experience and location. Takes less than 2 minutes.

3

Our Team Calls You Within 24 Hours

A MAINDSOURCE coordinator reviews your application and calls you directly. They will discuss the role, confirm your availability and answer any questions.

4

Interview & Document Verification

Depending on the role, we will schedule a walk-in interview or telephonic screening. Document verification (ID, address proof) is done with our team.

5

Deployment Within 72 Hours of Selection

Once selected, joining formalities are completed and you are deployed to the employer. PF and ESIC registration is handled by MAINDSOURCE on your behalf.
