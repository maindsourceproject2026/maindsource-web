Our Three Pillars

How We Put This Into Practice

Three concrete commitments that define how MAINDSOURCE approaches Women Empowerment — not in words, but in action.

01
⚖️

Equal Opportunity Placement

We actively place women candidates across all sectors — including male-dominated industries like manufacturing, logistics, and electronics. Equal capability deserves equal opportunity.

Gender-neutral screening process for all roles
Active outreach to women candidates in rural Karnataka and AP
Advocacy with client companies to adopt inclusive hiring policies
Zero tolerance for discriminatory job requirements in our listings
02
🎓

Skill Development & Training

We invest in pre-placement training that equips women with the technical and soft skills needed to thrive in their roles from day one.

Role-specific technical skill modules before placement
Workplace communication and confidence workshops
Safety and rights awareness for factory environments
Ongoing upskilling support post-placement
03
🏠

Safe & Inclusive Workplaces

We only partner with employers who meet our baseline standards for safe, respectful, and inclusive working environments.

Employer vetting for workplace safety compliance
Anti-harassment policy confirmation before partnerships
90-day check-in calls with every placed woman candidate
Direct escalation line for workplace concerns

Why We Do This

The Business Case for Women in the Workforce

Empowering women is not just the right thing to do — it is the smart thing to do. Companies with gender-diverse workforces consistently outperform those without.

📈

Higher Retention Rates

Women candidates placed in the right environment show consistently higher retention rates than industry averages.

⭐

Stronger Work Ethic

Women entering the workforce, especially from rural South India, bring dedication and discipline that employers consistently praise.

🌎

Community-Wide Impact

When a woman earns, an entire family benefits. Our placements create ripple effects far beyond individual employment.

🏭

Wider Talent Pool for Employers

By removing gender bias from hiring, our clients access a significantly larger and often underutilised pool of qualified candidates.

Our Commitment

What We Pledge to Every Woman We Work With

These are not policies on a wall — they are promises we keep in every engagement.

🛡️

Safe Placement Guarantee

We only place women with employers who meet our workplace safety standards. No exceptions.

📞

Post-Placement Support Line

Every placed woman has a direct contact at MAINDSOURCE for any workplace concern during the first 90 days.

💰

Equal Pay Advocacy

We negotiate equal compensation for women candidates and flag any discriminatory pay structures with clients.

🤲

No Judgement Registration

We welcome women at any stage of their career journey — first job, returning to work, or changing industries.

🏠

Accommodation Assistance

For women relocating to Bangalore, we assist with identifying safe, verified accommodation options near the workplace.

📋

Transparent Process

We explain every step of the placement process clearly, in the candidate’s preferred language where possible.
